Shipping & Returns





Shipping Policy

At FootWearX, we strive to provide fast and reliable shipping. Here’s what you need to know about our shipping policy:

  • Orders are processed within 1-2 business days.
  • We offer free standard shipping on all orders within the country.
  • Expedited shipping options are available at an additional cost. Estimated delivery time: 2-3 business days.
  • International shipping is available. Shipping charges vary based on destination and size of the order.
  • You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has been shipped.
  • Shipping times may vary depending on the location, holidays, and weather conditions.

Returns & Exchanges

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return or exchange it under our returns policy:

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days of receiving your order.
  • Items must be in unused condition, with original tags, and in the original packaging.
  • Return shipping is free for all domestic orders. International returns are at the customer’s expense.
  • Once we receive the returned item, we will process your refund within 7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • If you'd like to exchange an item, simply return the item and place a new order through our website.
  • Some items, such as personalized or clearance items, may be ineligible for return. Please check the product description for return eligibility.
