How to Measure Your Foot

To find your correct shoe size, follow these simple steps:

  1. Place your foot on a piece of paper and trace its outline.
  2. Measure the length from the heel to the tip of your longest toe.
  3. Use the measurements in the chart below to find your shoe size.
